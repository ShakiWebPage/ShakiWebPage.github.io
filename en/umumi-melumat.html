General Information

Sheki is one of the oldest cities in Azerbaijan. It has been a republican city since January 4, 1963. In medieval sources, his name is Shaki, Sheka, Shakki, etc. called as. The city of Sheki has long been called Nukha. The powers of the head of the Sheki city executive power also apply to the Sheki region. Sheki is located in the north-west of the country, on the southern slope of the Greater Caucasus Mountains, on the left bank of the Kish River, at an altitude of 632 m above sea level, 370 km from the capital Baku, far from the central regions - in an isolated area. There are 71 settlements in the district. 1 of them is a city, 2 are settlements, 68 are villages. As a result of charming nature, unique historical and architectural monuments, developed craftsmanship, protection of rich historical and cultural heritage, Sheki has become an important tourist region of Azerbaijan.






Coat of arms

The coat of arms depicts an octagonal grid element reminiscent of the moon and stars in the middle against the background of the Sheki fortress. A white horse is shown in front of the network. "Shaki" is written in white on the silk fabric below the coat of arms. The tower and silk fabric are decorated with different shades of blue, and the network is decorated with blue, red and green colors, as in the Azerbaijani flag. The castle usually covers the main official in the coat of arms, as it is defensive in nature. The moon and an eight-pointed star are engraved on the Azerbaijani flag, which is reflected in the network element. The network element is shown as a symbol of Sheki, the land of artists. Mayor

Elkhan Zabir oglu Usubov - Head of Sheki City Executive Power. Elkhan Usubov was born on May 26, 1960 in Mingachevir and received his secondary education there. He is an electrical engineer and economist by profession. He is the author of dozens of rationalization and scientific proposals. He started his career in Sheki, then engaged in business. From December 2007 to May 23, 2011 he served as the head of the Executive Power of Agstafa region. On May 23, 2011 by the Order of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan he was appointed as the head of Sheki City Executive Power.
